Introduction to Computer Science

Computer Science is a section of science that revolves around the theory of computation and architecture/design of a computer. The main goal of computer science is to apply different principles of mathematics, engineering, and logical thinking to a variety of computer functions. For instance, mathematical algorithms are created and implemented in computers with the purpose of completing a task more efficiently and effectively than a human can. Along with that the design of computer science deals with creating effective hardware and software to increase the effectiveness of new technology.

A Computer Scientist is a person that applies the principles of mathematics, engineering, and logical thinking to technology such as computers. Computer Scientist have a variety of skills from creating software and applications to resolving issues with hardware.However, this is just one of many jobs and career opportunities in the computer science field.

Median Pay In US

The median income in 2020 for a Computer Scientist according to BLS is $126,830 per year, around $60.97 per hour. The job outlook starting in 2019-2029 is expected to be 15%, which is a much faster growth rate for jobs than the average 4%.

Information Technology Specialist

An information technology specialist also known as an IT specialist or IT professional is a person that oversees technical support for a business or organization. The task of an IT specialist includes the following

List of Task

  • Installation and activation of computer hardware and software
  • Offer technical support to the business and the staff by fixing hardware issues
  • Troubleshooting and diagnosing technical or software issues
  • Implement security measures to protect the organizations information
  • Analyze and report the cost of possible computer maintenance and repair
  • Maintain and keep track of the information passed through the Computer network

    • Median Pay In US

    The median income in 2020 for an Information Specialist according to BLS is $55,510 per year; around $26.69 per hour. The job outlook starting in 2019-2029 is expected to be 8%, which is a much faster growth rate for jobs than the average 4%.

    Research Scientist

    A Computer and Information Research Scientist analyzes existing computer technology with the purpose of improving and developing new ways to innovate technology. Research Scientists use data gathered from a multitude of places to develop new technology and enhance existing technology. They are often finding ways to simplifying complex algorithms used in computing for a variety of fields such as business, medicine, robotics, and many others.

    As a research scientist the goal is to improve computer hardware and software by solving and simplifying complex algorithms. Some of the tasks that are completed to achieve that goal include the following:

    List of Task

  • Search for problems in computing and develop algorithms theories and models to reduce those problems.
  • Aid computer scientists, engineers, and programmers in the effort to solve complex computer algorithms.
  • Come up with new programming languages, and tools, and techniques for the purpose of making it easier for users to operate computers.
  • Create tests to determine the effectiveness of new software along with analyzing the data for possible improvements.

    • Median Pay In US

    The median income in 2020 for a Research Scientist according to BLS is $126,830 per year, around $60.97 per hour. The job outlook starting in 2019-2029 is expected to be 15%, which is a much faster growth rate for jobs than the average 4%.

    Web Developer

    Web Developers design, create, and maintain websites. Their responsibilities vary from a variety of technical tasks like programming as a front-end or back-end developer to other tasks such as testing site performance and capacity. Along with that they are responsible for making the website user friendly and function as to best accommodate the target audience of the website.

    A Web Developer uses a variety of programming languages to create a website such as HTML, XML, CSS, JavaScript, and others. As a web developer these are some of the responsibilities that come with the role:

    List of Task

  • A web developer must meet with clients to discuss details about design, functionality, target audience, and purpose for the website (is it for business, education, E-commerce, or something else).
  • Create a mock design of the website for review before coding it in a programming language such as HTML.
  • Work with the client to determine the information that the website will contain along with creating a user-friendly interface and navigation menus.
  • After completion of the website, have a way to monitor website traffic and client feedback to prove the website.

    • The task of a web developer can be split up into four main groups: the front-end developer, the back-end developer, website designer, and webmaster.

    Back-end developers are responsible for the overall technical construction of the website. They create the basic framework of the site and ensure that it functions as expected. Back-end web developers also establish procedures for allowing others to add new pages to the website and meet with management to discuss major changes to the site.

    Front-end developers create the technical features for a website’s look. They develop the site’s layout and integrate graphics, applications (such as a retail checkout tools), and other content. They also write web design programs in a variety of computer languages, such as HTML or JavaScript.

    Website designers are responsible for creating the look and feel of a website or interface with regard to photos, color, font type and size, graphics, and layout. They also are responsible for the functionality, usability, and compatibility of the website or interface.

    Webmasters maintain and update websites. They ensure that websites operate correctly, and they test for errors such as broken links. Many webmasters respond to user comments as well.

    Median Pay In US

    The median income in 2020 for a web developer according to BLS is $77,200 per year, around $37.12 per hour. The job outlook starting in 2019-2029 is expected to be 8%, which is a much faster growth rate for jobs than the average 4%.

    Network Architect

    A Network Architect is responsible for designing and building different data communication networks. Some of the networks they are in charge of designing include local area networks (LAN), wide area networks (WAN), extranets, and intranets. The main goal of a network architect is to work with companies to design and deploy a network that best suits the organizations business plan. However, it is also important to design the network in such a way as to be flexible enough to change in the future. Some of the duties a network architect is responsible for include:

    List of Task

  • Doing research and having an extensive knowledge of the organization's business plan to provide them with the network that will best help to achieve their goal.
  • A network architect must create a plan that shows the overall layout of the network configuration and then present them to the organization’s management.
  • When designing the network, it is important to consider the information traveling through the network and the level of security needed to protect that data.
  • Perform upgrades and maintenance on the network with the purpose of make sure it is working as intended and the data is secure.

    • Median Pay In US

    The median income in 2020 for a Network Architect according to BLS is $116,780 per year, around $56.14 per hour. The job outlook starting in 2019-2029 is expected to be 5%, which is a slightly faster growth rate for jobs than the average 4%.

    Software Developer

    Software Developers are computer programmers that are responsible for analyzing the demands of the users and designing software to meet those demands. They often make different software for a multitude of devices such as computer, mobile phones, tablets, and gaming consoles. As a software developer these are some of the job responsibilities:

    List of Task

  • Research the needs of the user to design, test, and develop software to meet those needs.
  • Create a plan such as a flowchart to map out how the software will work on every level of the design.
  • During and after development of the software the developer must perform routine maintenance.
  • Support business organizations by recommending software upgrades that reduce existing technical issues.
  • Document every part of the software building process to use as a reference in the future.

    • Median Pay In US

    The median income in 2020 for a software developer according to BLS is $110,140 per year, around $52.95 per hour. The job outlook starting in 2019-2029 is expected to be 22%, which is a much faster growth rate for jobs than the average 4%.

    Database Administrator

    A Data Administrator also known as a DBA is in charge of creating and maintaining databases to store all types of information. That information can vary depending on what type of organization they are working. For instance, a hospital would require a database for medical records. The main goal of a database administrator is to make sure the database is accurate, and data is readily available for data analysts and other authorized users to use as well as secure from unapproved users.

    List of Task

  • Ensure that organizational data are secure.
  • Back up and restore data to prevent data loss.
  • Identify user needs to create and administer databases.
  • Ensure that databases operate efficiently and without error.
  • Maintain databases and update permissions.

    • Median Pay In US

    The median income in 2020 for data administrator according to BLS is $98,860 per year, around $47.53 per hour. The job outlook starting in 2019-2029 is expected to be 10%, which is a much faster growth rate for jobs than the average 4%.

    Information Security Analyst

    Information security analysts plan and carry out security measures to protect an organization’s computer networks and systems. Their responsibilities are continually expanding as the number of cyberattacks increases.

    List of Task

  • Monitor their organization’s networks for security breaches and investigate a violation when one occurs.
  • Install and use software, such as firewalls and data encryption programs, to protect sensitive information.
  • Prepare reports that document security breaches and the extent of the damage caused by the breaches.
  • Conduct penetration testing, which is when analysts simulate attacks to look for vulnerabilities in their systems before they can be exploited.
  • Develop security standards and best practices for their organization.

    • Median Pay In US

    The median income in 2020 for a Information Security Analyst according to BLS is $103,590 per year, around $49.80 per hour. The job outlook starting in 2019-2029 is expected to be 31%, which is a much faster growth rate for jobs than the average 4%.
